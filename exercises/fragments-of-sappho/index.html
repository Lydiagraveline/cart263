FRAGMENTS OF SAPPHO

This webpage is an interactive way to read Anne Carson's translation of Sappho from her book If Not, Winter. Sappho lived on the island of Lesbos from bout 630 B.C. She was a musical genius who devoted her life to composing and performing songs. Of the nine books of lyrics Sappho is said to have composed, only one poem has survived complete. All the rest are fragments. Experience the drama of trying to read a papyrus torn in half or riddled with holes. Keep up with the actively disintegrating text by hovering your mouse over the fading fragments.
